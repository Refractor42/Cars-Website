Popular Models

  • The Tesla Model S is a fully electric luxury sedan that was first introduced in 2012. It has a range of up to 373 miles (depending on the version) on a single charge and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in as little as 2.4 seconds. It also has a variety of advanced features such as a 17-inch touchscreen display and autopilot functionality.
  • The BMW M3 is a high-performance version of the BMW 3-Series sedan. The current generation (G80) was introduced in 2020, it's available with a twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter I-6 engine that generates 473 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. It's able to accelerate from 0-60 mph in 3.8 seconds. It comes with advanced suspension, braking and aerodynamics systems to handle that power.
  • The Ford Mustang is a classic American muscle car that was first introduced in 1964. The current generation was introduced in 2018. It's available with a variety of engine options, including a 2.3-liter EcoBoost 4-cylinder and a 5.0-liter V8. It has a horsepower ranging from 310 to 480 depending on the version, and it's able to accelerate from 0-60 mph in as little as 4 seconds.
